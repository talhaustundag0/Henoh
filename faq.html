FAQ

What's the
top speed of
the bike?

The X-1 tops out at 22 miles per hour for pedal assist. This is the legal speed limit for street legal e-bikes in most areas. With extra pedaling or going downhill you can go faster.

What's the
range of the
X-1?

Typically range is about 30 miles per charge, but can be up to 50+ miles, depending on cargo and amount of pedaling. The LCD screen shows the battery level.

Am I a good
height for
the bike?

Generally, we recommend a height range between 5’4” and 6’4”.
